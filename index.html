We are Hiring: Podcast Producer

DAY-TO-DAY RESPONSIBILITIES: (Part-time 10hrs per week/ Time block: 10 am – 12 pm PST (tentative) )

The Podcast Producer will play a pivotal role in creating and promoting the Clients podcast. This position ensures smooth production, consistent content quality, and engaging promotions across multiple platforms. Responsibilities span the entire production process, including pre-production, production, post-production, and marketing on Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, and other platforms.

Qualifications:

  • Audio Production & Editing Expertise: Proficient with audio tools like Audacity and Riverside, ensuring each episode is clear, polished, and maintains a high production quality.
  • Guest and Content Coordination: Experience in managing guest logistics—from scheduling to gathering bios—and preparing insightful questions and content outlines that align with listener interests.
  • Social Media & Content Promotion: Familiarity in social media management across platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube, with a talent for creating and scheduling engaging promotional content.
  • Email Marketing Proficiency: Expertise with email marketing tools like Keap, adept at crafting campaigns that drive engagement and tracking performance metrics to optimize results.
  • Performance Monitoring & Strategic Adjustment: Knowledge in tracking key podcast metrics, such as downloads, social engagement, and email effectiveness, and using insights to refine and grow the podcast’s reach and impact.

Responsibilities

Pre-Production

Guest Management

  • Schedule recordings by sending calendar invites, gathering guest bios, and collecting high-resolution photos.
  • Collaborate with the host to develop 10 sample questions aligned with audience interests.
  • Ensure all tech setups (e.g., Riverside, Audacity) are fully functional.

Content Planning

  • Assist in scripting solo episodes or creating episode outlines.
  • Prepare promotional content, such as social media teasers, for upcoming episodes.

Production

Recording Setup

  • Use platforms like Audacity or Riverside to manage recordings, ensuring high-quality audio.
  • Oversee recording sessions and monitor for technical issues.

Recording Setup

  • Manage uploads to chosen platforms (e.g., Libsyn, Captivate, or Castos), ensuring the podcast reaches all major directories.

Post-Production

Editing

  • Edit episodes to remove mistakes or sensitive parts, add intro/outro music, and ensure high production quality.
  • Create highlight sound clips for social media promotion.

Show Notes & Metadata

  • Draft engaging show notes summarizing key points and insights to attract listeners.
  • Add relevant keywords for SEO and platform optimization.

Promotion

Social Media Promotion

  • Create and distribute promotional materials for Facebook, Instagram, and the blog.
  • Engage listeners and followers through regular posts, tag guests, and encourage interaction.

Email Campaigns

  • Design and track email campaigns using Keap, monitoring open rates and click-through rates to improve performance.

Content Strategy

  • Consistently create promotional content for the blog (WordPress) and maintain uploads on YouTube and Meta Business Suite.

Ad Hoc Promotional Strategies

  • Pitch RetailMaven’s podcast to other podcast owners for potential collaboration.

Ongoing Monitoring & Analytics

Performance Tracking

  • Track listens, downloads, social engagement, and email campaign metrics to evaluate podcast growth.
  • Adjust podcast strategies based on performance insights.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

  • Episode Timeliness: All episodes are edited, uploaded, and promoted on time.
  • High-Quality Production: Maintain high audio quality and error-free episodes.
  • Audience Growth: Steady increase in podcast downloads, listens, and social media engagement.
  • Email Campaign Performance: Increase in email campaign open and click-through rates.
  • Platform Optimization: Effective use of RSS feed platforms and regular content updates on Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube.
